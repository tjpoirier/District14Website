Meeting List Codes
B
 Big Book meeting
The "Big Book" (Alcoholics Anonymous) is read and discussed at these meetings.
BX
 Big Book Step Study meeting
The twelve steps of recovery as laid out in the "Big Book", are read and discussed at these meetings.
C
 Closed meeting
Attendance at closed meetings is limited to persons who have a desire to stop drinking.
D
 Discussion meeting
Persons who have a desire to stop drinking discuss their problems, as they relate to alcohol.
F
 French speaking meeting
G
 Gay and Lesbian meeting
H
 Handicapped Accessible meeting
O
 Open meeting - welcome all visitors
S
 Speaker meeting
One or more A.A. speakers share their Experience, Strength, and Hope.
X
 Step meeting
The book "Twelve Steps and Twelve Traditions" is read and discussed at these meetings.
Z
 Smoking Allowed meeting
+
 Hearing Impaired meeting
&
 Spanish speaking meeting
*
 Al-Anon meeting in the same location at the same time
