NATURE'S QUIET ROVERS
A monthly activity that combines a meeting, a short period of
meditation, and an outdoor activity set in nature. As such, each
meeting is at a "roving" venue. Typically scheduled for
the 1st Weekend of the month at 1pm, on either Saturday or Sunday.
Changes in venue due to weather will be emailed 24 hrs in advance.
To join the email list, email to NQR@SeacoastAA.org
